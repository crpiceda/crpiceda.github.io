Outreach

Co-editor in chief EGU Geodynamics division

Since 2023, I am co-editor in chief of the Geodynamics division blog of the European Geoscience Union. I co-manage a team of 15 editors, columnists and illustrators who are PhDs students, postdocs and senior scientists. We publish weekly blogs about Geodynamics, related disciplines and Academia. In addition, I edit the blogs of guest authors and write blogs myself
